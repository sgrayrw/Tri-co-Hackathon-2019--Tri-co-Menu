Dining Center

Students eating inside of the Dining Center.

The Dining Center (DC) is a traditional "all you care to eat" facility with a variety of menus to fit today's student preferences.

Typical offerings include "made from scratch" recipes developed by our own chefs: entrees and soups (including vegan and vegetarian options), fresh fruits and vegetables, and a variety of gourmet composed salads. We strive to use local and organic ingredients whenever possible.
